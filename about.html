IMPORTANT MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

This system provides first-aid guidance only and is NOT a substitute for professional medical care. Always seek immediate medical attention for emergencies.

The AI does NOT diagnose conditions. It provides guidance based on symptoms described. If in doubt, call emergency services immediately.

How This System Works

1. Voice-First Design: The system speaks automatically to guide you through emergencies, especially when you cannot look at the screen.

2. AI Severity Assessment: Our engine classifies emergencies as Mild, Moderate, or Severe based on described symptoms.

3. Auto Emergency Call: For severe emergencies, the system automatically calls local emergency services after a 5-second countdown.

4. Multi-Language Support: Works in English, Urdu, and Roman Urdu with auto-detection of language.

Privacy & Data Safety

• We do NOT store your voice recordings or personal information.

• Location data is used only to find nearby help and is not stored.

• Emergency messages only include essential information: situation, location, and time.

• All processing happens locally in your browser when possible.

Permissions Explained

• Microphone: For voice input when you describe the emergency.

• Location: To find nearby hospitals and send your location to emergency services.

• Phone/SMS: To automatically call and message emergency contacts (with your confirmation).
